🔢 Linear Equations

Slope-Intercept Form:

Easy: Find y when x = 2 in y = 3x + 1

📊 Basic Set Operations

Union:
A ∪ B = {x | x ∈ A or x ∈ B}
n(A ∪ B) = n(A) + n(B) - n(A ∩ B)
Easy: A = {1,2,3}, B = {3,4,5}
A ∪ B = {1,2,3,4,5}
n(A ∪ B) = 3 + 3 - 1 = 5
Hard: In a survey of 100 students: 60 study Math, 40 study Physics, and 20 study both. How many study at least one subject?
n(M ∪ P) = 60 + 40 - 20 = 80 students
Intersection:
A ∩ B = {x | x ∈ A and x ∈ B}
n(A ∩ B) = n(A) + n(B) - n(A ∪ B)
Easy: A = {1,2,3}, B = {3,4,5}
A ∩ B = {3}
n(A ∩ B) = 1
Hard: In a group of 50 people, 30 speak English, 25 speak French, and 10 speak both. How many speak only one language?
Only English: 30 - 10 = 20
Only French: 25 - 10 = 15
Total = 20 + 15 = 35
Complement:
A' = {x | x ∉ A}
n(A') = n(U) - n(A)
Easy: U = {1,2,3,4,5}, A = {1,2}
A' = {3,4,5}
n(A') = 5 - 2 = 3
Hard: In a school of 200 students, 120 play football, 50 play chess, and 30 play both. How many play neither?
n(F ∪ C) = 120 + 50 - 30 = 140
n(F ∪ C)' = 200 - 140 = 60

🔢 Advanced Set Properties

De Morgan's Laws:
(A ∪ B)' = A' ∩ B'
(A ∩ B)' = A' ∪ B'
Easy: Verify with A = {1,2}, B = {2,3}, U = {1,2,3,4}
(A ∪ B)' = {4} = A' ∩ B' = {3,4} ∩ {1,4} = {4}
Hard: Prove De Morgan's Law using Venn diagrams
Shading (A∪B)' matches shading A'∩B' in all cases
Three Set Cardinality:
n(A∪B∪C) = n(A) + n(B) + n(C) - n(A∩B) - n(B∩C) - n(A∩C) + n(A∩B∩C)
Hard: In a survey of 100 people: 60 like Tea, 40 like Coffee, 30 like Milk, 20 like Tea & Coffee, 15 like Coffee & Milk, 10 like Tea & Milk, and 5 like all three. How many like none?
n(T∪C∪M) = 60+40+30-20-15-10+5 = 90
None = 100 - 90 = 10

↔ Ratio Basics

Ratio Definition:
a : b = a/b

Hard: Divide $1200 in ratio 3:4:5

⇨ Direct Proportion

Direct Proportion:
y ∝ x ⇒ y = kx
Easy: If y ∝ x and y=12 when x=4, find y when x=7
k = 12/4 = 3 ⇒ y = 3×7 = 21
Hard: The cost of painting is directly proportional to area. 50m² costs $150. What's the cost for 80m²?
k = 150/50 = $3/m² ⇒ Cost = 3×80 = $240

⇦ Inverse Proportion

Inverse Proportion:
y ∝ 1/x ⇒ y = k/x
Easy: If y ∝ 1/x and y=6 when x=4, find y when x=3
k = 6×4 = 24 ⇒ y = 24/3 = 8
Hard: The time to complete a task is inversely proportional to workers. 5 workers take 12 days. How long for 8 workers?
k = 5×12 = 60 ⇒ Time = 60/8 = 7.5 days

⇆ Continued Proportion

Continued Proportion:
a : b = b : c ⇒ b² = a×c
Easy: Find mean proportional between 4 and 16
x² = 4×16 ⇒ x = 8
Hard: If a, b, c are in continued proportion and a=9, c=16, find b
9 : b = b : 16 ⇒ b² = 144 ⇒ b = 12

⇄ Compound Proportion

Compound Proportion:
If a ∝ b and a ∝ 1/c ⇒ a ∝ b/c ⇒ a = k(b/c)
Hard: If 5 workers can build 2 houses in 10 days, how many days will 8 workers take to build 3 houses?
Days ∝ Houses/Workers ⇒ 10 = k(2/5) ⇒ k = 25
Days = 25×(3/8) = 9.375 days
